Mounts of Mayhem is a game drop that was released on December 9, 2025[1] as Java Edition 1.21.11 and Bedrock Edition 1.21.130. Its theme is "mounts and combat". It introduces the spears, netherite horse armor, nautiluses, nautilus armor, zombie horses spawning naturally as zombie horsemen, zombie nautiluses, camel husks, and parched. It was announced at Minecraft LIVE – September 2025
